WHAT IS THE HAMSTER SOCIETY

The Hamster Society is a collection of 10,000 Hamster NFTs – unique, randomly-generated digital collectibles on the Ethereum blockchain.
Whilst the Hamsters are one family they can be distinguished between The Genesis Generation - the first 2,500 Hamsters with the original special characteristic and exclusive perks, as well as 7,500 of The New Age Generation all with a unique look.

HOW IT ALL STARTED

The story dates back to times long past, when mankind lived in peace and harmony with hamsters. The aspect of their lives that Hamsters kept secret from us, was that they were the ones looking after our planet. For decades, humanity was slowly destroying our planet and hamsters knew it wouldn’t be long until it wasn’t habitable anymore.

As a result, they decided to gather supplies and build a new society underground. The first hamster to roll off to the underground was Hamso, he was the founder of The Hamster Society where hamsters lived together and expanded their Kingdom. After years of living in their Society, the hamsters realised that they have an empty spot in their hearts. They missed the love and care of the children that they once belonged to. Even if we aren’t taking care of the world, the children are not to blame.

Because of this, they decided to give humanity one last chance and come back outside after all these years. The reason behind it was simple, they had an aim to help this planet by teaching the young generation how to take care of the environment and help the world they are living in, because unfortunately, we don’t get a second chance. They were the first ones to realise and that’s why they are our last hope.

WHAT'S GONNA HAPPEN AFTER WE SELL OUT?

Whilst the team have been working away making the project a reality we always keep our eye on the road ahead. We have been developing various additions and mechanisms to add value to our patrons; This includes a Staking mechanism where holders can earn tokens - These tokens can then be used in our Play to Earn Arcade where you will be able to win various benefits and prizes.

Aside from this, as the society grows, we will be looking at expanding the DAO element to be able to capitalise on any opportunistic investment proposals that may arise - This allows holders to realise a much higher value return on items than they may have got individually - As an example we have recently seen a DAO come close to buying an original copy of the declaration of independence, or Murat Paks recent Merge project where DAOs were formed to specifically buy as many units as possible as a group to capitalise on the additional benefits.

HOW ARE WE GOING TO MAKE A CHANGE?

Hamsters came to teach us about things long forgotten and make a change. To fulfill their purpose, we collect part of the money raised into our community wallet and decide upon the causes we would like to focus on. We focus on two main causes we will be supporting – increasing awareness about sustainability among future generations (aged 6-13) and by direct investments from the money raised into charities/green projects directed to make a change for a better future. These joint efforts will continue even after our initial donation of US$200,000+ (60 ETH) as we strive to make the biggest impact possible.
